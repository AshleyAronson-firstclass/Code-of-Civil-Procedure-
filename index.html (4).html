About the Compiler

About Jeremy J. Salvador.

Jeremy J. Salvador is a Certified Public Accountant, Certified Valuation Analyst, and Certified Fraud Examiner who serves as a forensic accounting expert witness at Miod and Company, LLP. His work regularly places him inside California civil litigation — from discovery through trial — where precise, current statutory text is not a convenience but a requirement.

That day-to-day need for a clean, reliable, portable copy of the Code of Civil Procedure is what led to compiling this edition. Rather than a commentary or a study guide, this is the statutory text itself: organized, current, and split into two volumes sized for a desk, a bag, or a courtroom.

The California Code of Civil Procedure, 2026 Edition is published by League Publishing, and follows the same annual compilation approach as the California Civil Code, 2026 Edition.
